About Music Programs

Our music programs inspire creativity, expression, and collaboration through exploration of rhythm, melody, and musical forms. We believe in creating a supportive environment where students can discover the joy of making music together.

From learning to play instruments to understanding music theory and composition, students develop both technical skills and appreciation for diverse musical traditions. Our approach emphasizes active participation, creative exploration, and the connections between music and other areas of learning.

Areas of Focus

  • Instrumental Music
  • Vocal Music and Singing
  • Music Theory and Composition
  • World Music and Cultural Traditions
  • Music Technology and Production

Featured Programs

  • Homeschool Music Ensemble
  • Songwriting Workshop
  • Music Exploration for Young Children
  • Digital Music Creation
