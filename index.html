Home Education in New England

The home education scene in New England is full of vibrant learning opportunities for pre-k to 12 learners. The collaborative highlights the people who provide learning spaces, educational and recreational programs, and run groups that contribute to the homeschooling lifestyle.

How to Join NEHE Collaborative

Becoming part of our community is easy! Here's how you can join NEHE - Email us at nehecollaborative@gmail.com

Membership Requirements

To be a member of NEHE, you must be one of the following:

  • Operate a education program or offer educational services
  • Licensed tradesperson
  • Run an educational or recreational group
  • Author or illustrator
  • As part of membership, we send an email inviting you to a webinar, skillshare or workshop
