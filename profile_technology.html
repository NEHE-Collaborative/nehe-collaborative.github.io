Technology Programs

Technology programs help young learners develop digital literacy and computational thinking skills. Empowering students to become creators, not just consumers, of technology through hands-on projects and guided exploration, children learn to understand technological concepts and navigate the digital landscape.

Areas of Focus

  • Programming and Coding Fundamentals
  • Digital Media Creation
  • Robotics and Automation
  • Web Development
  • Game Design
