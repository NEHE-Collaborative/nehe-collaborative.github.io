About Let Children Play LLC

Let Children Play provides a space for kids to play and parents to connect. They are building a non-competitive, inclusive community based on the importance of play as a critical component of childhood. At LCP, they empower parents to learn strategies on promoting self-directed play experiences.

Thanks to Leland Wild Foundation, their home is nestled in a beautiful part of the forest amongst the trails. Leland Wild supports nearly 200 acres of wildlife habitat. Playing in the forest allows children and families to relax and talk about the everyday pressures of balancing technology and outdoor play time.
