About The Forest Box for Kids

The Forest Box for Kids provides monthly nature education and activities delivered right to your door. Each box is carefully curated by Ricardo Sierra, who has taught wilderness skills to over 10,000 children for more than 30 years.

Their nature education curriculum has been awarded the Parents' Pick Award as one of the best educational materials for children. The Forest Box helps children develop a meaningful connection with the natural world through transformational experiences and hands-on activities.

To learn more about Ricardo Sierra, visit foresteducator.com/my-story.
